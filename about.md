# About the Workshop

Welcome to the **qStack: International Workshop on Quantum Software Systems**. This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners to discuss the future of quantum computing stacks, from hardware to software and applications.

## Overview
As quantum hardware scales up, the software stack becomes increasingly critical. This workshop aims to explore:
- Quantum Operating Systems
- Real-time Control
- Error Correction Decoding
- Compiler Optimizations
- Distributed Quantum Computing

We invite submissions and participation from the entire community.
